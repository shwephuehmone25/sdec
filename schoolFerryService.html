School Ferry Service of SDEC

  • School Ferry Service

    • School Ferry Service

    In order to avoid traffic jam around the school environment, the school provides Ferries for the convenience of the students. Those students who live in the outskirts or in the lately set up new townships may come in their own car. Otherwise, the students who live in nearby townships are strongly advised to take school ferries which are well-facilitated for the convenience for the students.
