Vision, Mission and Aims of SDEC

  • Vision, Mission and Aims
    • Our Vision

    To empower students with high-quality education by challenging them to excel intellectually, emotionally, physically, culturally, socially, and aesthetically through integrated teaching with a global focus and a vision for tomorrow.

    Our Mission

    To enable students to become globally competitive Myanmar citizens through:

    1. Nurturing independent students to reach their full potential.
    2. Fostering appreciation for Myanmar tradition and culture.
    3. Preparing learners to become responsible and educated citizens.
    Our Aims

    Our 10 Primary Aims for SDEC students:

    1. Focus on well-rounded development.
    2. Promote inclusive education.
    3. Encourage appreciation of Myanmar heritage.
    4. Develop 21st-century skills.
    5. Ensure bilingual proficiency.
    6. Promote physical education and sportsmanship.
    7. Encourage environmental awareness.
    8. Foster global citizenship.
    9. Build civic responsibility.

    Intellectual growth aim includes development in students’ critical thinking, civility, and friendly relations. Emotional growth mainly aims at maturity in feelings, interests and values. Practical skills development aim includes competency in bilingual, both English and Myanmar languages, Maths, Natural Science, Social Studies and Information Technology skills. Spiritual growth development in whole hearted soul-enrichment. It is one of the latest addition for education aims. They must be ready and qualified to get admissions to Universities and Colleges in and abroad. They must be able to transfer to any appropriate or equivalent grade in the public schools if need arises in accordance with the Education Ministry’s Private Education Law prescriptions.
