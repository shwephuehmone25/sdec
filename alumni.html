Alumni of SDEC

  • Alumni

    • Alumni

    SDEC International School has produced a great number of outstanding Alumni whose success stories are motivating, exemplary, stimulating, and inspiring for all other incumbent students of different Key Stages.
    Therefore, here are some success stories of our Alumni about which SDEC is presenting in a deserved pride.
    Aye Thae Yu Win
    Aye Thae Yu Win @ Soni had been one of our Exemplary Alumni who successfully accomplished IGCSE Exam with excellent grades in 2020 having earned 5(A*)s and 2(A)s. She received a 50% scholarship for Pre-University Program( Foundation in Science) at International Medical University (Malaysia) and was able to enter Undergraduate Course with CGPA 4.0 and is currently a successful Sophomore (Second Year Student) in Semester 4.
    Wai Yan Phone Naing
    Wai Yan Phone Naing had also been one of Our Exemplary Alumni who successfully accomplished IGCSE Exam with excellent grades in 2021 having earned 5(A*)s and 2(A)s. He applied for the Foundation in Biomedical Science at the University of Western Australia and was granted AUD 10,000 as Foundation Scholarship, and AUD 45,000 for Progression Scholarship.
    Chit Snow Thin
    Chit Snow Thin had been one of our Exemplary Alumni who, too, successfully accomplished IGCSE Exam with excellent grades in 2021 having earned Six (9)s all(A*). She applied for Pre-University Program (Foundation in Science) at MDIS_ Management Development Institute of Singapore and was granted Scholarship Achievement for Biomedical Science.
    Wint Thawdar Lin
    Likewise, Wint Thawdar Lin had been one our Exemplary Alumni who successfully accomplished IGCSE Exam with excellent grades in 2022 having earned (A*) in all seven subjects. She had achieved Academic Merit Scholarship of 50% to study Foundation Program at Trinity College, Australia and aspired to study for Bachelor of Biomedical Science at the University of Melbourne.
