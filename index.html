Principal's Welcome

SDEC Welcome message from the Principal

SDEC Welcome message from the Principal On behalf of everyone at SDEC International School, it is my pleasure to extend my warm welcome to our school. Our priority is to provide a safe, secure and engaging learning environment. The International School offers 21 st century learning method which is based on the student-centred learning system. This will encourage the students with motivating environment who want to access the world of high academic achievements through critical thinking, challenging problem-solving, creativity, and innovation skills from the project-oriented lessons.

  • Our rich programme of extracurricular provision provides opportunity for students to develop and flourish in sports, arts, music and academia, where they can develop key life skills including confidence, teamwork and leadership.
  • The school curriculum is engaged to UK International Qualification Curriculum framework approved by Pearson UK, ( Edexel Academic Qualifications) since 2019. The practical tools are also upgrading with up-to-date learning methodologies and resources. We, SDEC introduced and trained all secondary students to be academically well qualified through Cambridge Curriculum since 2016.
  • In the near future the school is going to introduce another UK Cambridge programme. Students of IGCSE are allowed to make a choice for examination between EDEXCEL and Cambridge International Examination (CIE).

Both examinations are highly respected and have equal value in regards to further studies or employment...

About Us

Skills Development Education Centre (SDEC) was established in 1999 and started with preschool classes. Since then, it has gained the trust and support of the parents.

  • Today, we have become one of the popular international schools in Myanmar providing quality education.
  • SDEC is now running preschool, primary and secondary levels with expert, well-qualified and experienced teachers.
  • SDEC also has Summer Classes for English Proficiency and IGCSE and IAL Exam Preparation Classes.

Our Vision

To enable students become globally competitive Myanmar citizens through:

To empower students with high quality education by challenging them to excel intellectually, emotionally, physically, culturally, socially and aesthetically through integrated teaching with a global focus and a vision for tomorrow.

Our Mission

To enable students become globally competitive Myanmar citizens through:

  • Nurturing independent students to reach their full potential with standardized balanced international Curriculum and up-to-date teaching approaches.
  • Fostering globally competitive and civilized students to appreciate Myanmar tradition and culture.
  • Preparing all learners develop into educated, productive, responsible and involved citizens in an ever-changing world..

Our Aims

In terms of Teaching & Learning, there are 10 Primary Aims for our SDEC students as follow:

1. To nurture with focus on all round, balanced development especially in terms of intellectual and emotional dimensions.
2. To develop inclusive education by promoting students’ learning equally despite of any differences in disability and language.
